Know more about Attendance Management System (Atmas)

  1. Home
  2. About

ATMAS is a web based software designed to manage attendance very easily. With this, the teachers can record the attendance of the students and store the information of the students. ATMAS enables each student to know your attendance details accurately and understand their attendance status.
Different features are allowed for each type of user. Teachers will have facility to make some changes in student details and record attendance. Students will have the facility to view their current attendance status. Attendance status of all students can be known in Principal Profile. Each user will have a unique username and password to access their profile.
The admin profile cannot be accessed by other users. Accessing the admin profile and altering the information in any way is an offence.
